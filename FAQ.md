## About ZamAI

### What is ZamAI?
ZamAI is a modular AI platform and toolkit designed for:
- Pluggable, composable pipelines
- Configuration-driven experimentation
- Production-ready AI workflows
- Extensible plugin architecture

### What is ZamAI-Zeerak?
ZamAI-Zeerak is a companion module to ZamAI that provides:
- Inference optimization utilities
- Real-time observability hooks
- Evaluation and regression harness
- Metadata enrichment capabilities
